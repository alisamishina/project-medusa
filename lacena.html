After choosing the theme of our project, we had to find the painting in the ARCO ontology. For this reason, we created two SPARQL queries and selected the following link: https://dati.cultura.gov.it/lodview-arco/resource/HistoricOrArtisticProperty/0900289175.html «Testa di Medusa» (dipinto) by Merisi Michelangelo detto Caravaggio (sec. XVI) We analyzed the page thoroughly. Once we had examined the available data and explored the various ontologies provided by ArCo through its extensive catalogue, we noticed several gaps. We created a few SPARQL queries that revealed that some information concerning Testa di Medusa by Caravaggio was missing. To address this issue, we created RDF triples using LLMs and were able to enrich the Knowledge Graph. Then we applied three different prompting techniques across three LLMs: ChatGPT, Gemini and MistralAI about the chosen painting, whose outputs were subsequently analyzed and compared in their depth and way of providing information.
